# AfroLumens: Game Overview for Website

**A detailed guide to what AfroLumens is, how it works, why it matters now, and how players benefit.**

---

## What Is AfroLumens?

**AfroLumens** is a **Tap-to-Earn** game and engagement platform delivered as a **Telegram Mini App**. Players earn **ALM** (AfroLumens in-game currency) by tapping, completing activities, playing mini-games, and taking part in teams and leagues. ALM can be used in-app for upgrades, staking, marketplace, and P2P transfers, with a path to **Lumina ID**, wallet connection, and airdrops as the ecosystem grows.

The game combines:

- **Instant fun** – Tap to collect ALM, use energy, and watch your balance grow.
- **Strategy** – Upgrade your tap power, energy limit, and mine to earn more per hour.
- **Variety** – Daily rewards, puzzles, mini-games, quizzes, tasks, and weekly events.
- **Community** – Teams, leagues, championships, and competitions with real stakes and leaderboards.
- **Ownership** – Stake ALM for bonuses, trade on the P2P marketplace (ALM ↔ TON), and connect a TON wallet for future airdrops and Lumina ID.

No downloads beyond Telegram: open the app, accept terms, and start playing.

## How It Works

### 1. Core Loop: Tap to Earn

- **Tap** the main character to spend **energy** and earn **ALM** (points) per tap.
- **Energy** refills over time; you also get a limited number of **daily refills** (e.g. 6 per day with cooldown).
- **Multi-tap** and **energy limit** can be upgraded in the **Mine** by spending ALM, so each tap and each refill become more valuable.
- **Mine** level increases your **profit per hour**: you earn ALM passively (e.g. when the app is open or in the background), up to a **maximum idle time** (e.g. 3 hours) so there’s reason to return.

Progress is saved and synced with the server so your balance and upgrades persist across sessions.

### 2. Levels and Progression

- Your **total ALM** and **total taps** determine your **level**.
- Levels have names (e.g. **Baobab** → **Shaka** → **Mwami** → … → **Mansa**) and unlock better **referral bonuses** and status.
- Climbing the level ladder makes your account more valuable and improves rewards from inviting friends.

### 3. Earn Tab: Many Ways to Earn ALM

The **Earn** screen is the hub for non-tap activities. All of these reward **ALM** and/or **League Points (LP)** / **Team Points (TP)** where applicable:

| Activity | What it is | Typical benefit |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| **Karibu Daily** | Daily login calendar | Claim a reward each day; streak improves rewards. |
| **Decode** | Daily puzzle (e.g. dot–dash style) | ALM for solving (limited attempts per day). |
| **Matrix** | Daily 3-card combo game | ALM for correct combo (limited attempts). |
| **Spinza** | Tap & spin / wheel-style mini-game | Random ALM reward per spin. |
| **Mini games hub** | Umeme Run, Lucky Spin, Drums of the Baobab, Savanna Hunt, etc. | Extra ALM (often once per day per game). |
| **Quiz** | Multiple-choice blockchain/crypto quiz | ALM per correct answer + bonus if all correct. |
| **Weekly Event** | Weekly challenge + leaderboard | Tiered ALM rewards for taps, tasks, referrals. |
| **Tasks** | Follow channels, watch content, enter codes, etc. | ALM (and LP/TP) per completed task. |
| **Donation Center** | Donate ALM to charity | No direct ALM gain; community impact. |

Tasks are grouped (e.g. **Special**, **Leagues**, **Refs**) so players can focus on what they like.

### 4. Friends and Referrals

- **Invite friends** via your referral link (from the **Friends** tab).
- When referred users **join and accept terms**, you and they get **referral bonuses** (ALM).
- Higher **levels** increase the referral bonus amount.
- Referrals also feed into **League Points** for teams/leagues (e.g. +100 LP per referral).

### 5. Teams and Leagues

- **Team** – Small group (e.g. 2–7 members). Creating a team requires an **ALM commitment** (e.g. 1M ALM). Team members earn **Team Points (TP)** for daily login, tasks, events, referrals, mini-game wins, etc.
- **League** – Only **teams** can create or join a league. Creating a league requires a higher **ALM commitment** (e.g. 10M ALM). Members of a team that’s in a league earn **TP + League Points (LP)** (LP is a multiple of the same activities).
- **Tiers** – Leagues use tiers (e.g. **Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Legend**). Weekly leaderboards promote/demote players between tiers.
- **Championship** – Every few weeks (e.g. 8), top players (e.g. top 100 in Diamond & Legend) qualify for the **AfroLumens Championship** with special rewards and recognition.
- **Competitions** – **Team vs Team** and **League vs League** challenges: stake ALM, set a target (e.g. “first to X ALM wins”), and the winner takes the prize pool. Global joinable tasks let teams/leagues stake and compete for bonus rewards.

This gives competitive and social players clear goals and recurring events.

### 6. Mine: Upgrades and Staking

- **Upgrades** (purchased with ALM):
  - **Multi-tap** – More ALM per tap.
  - **Energy limit** – Bigger energy bar and more taps before refill.
  - **Mine** – Higher **profit per hour** (passive ALM).
- **Staking** – Lock ALM for a fixed period (e.g. 7 days to 180 days). At the end of the period you get your ALM back **plus a bonus %**. Longer and larger stakes typically offer higher bonus. Staking encourages holding and reduces sell pressure while rewarding commitment.

### 7. Collection

- View and track your **cards**, **achievements**, or other collectibles tied to progress and events (exact content is configurable per deployment).

### 8. Airdrop Tab: Wallet, Marketplace, and On-Chain

- **Connect TON wallet** (e.g. via TonConnect) to participate in:
  - **P2P Marketplace** – **Sell ALM for TON** or **buy ALM with TON**. Listings are escrowed; buyers pay TON and receive ALM.
  - **Transfers** – Send/receive ALM to other players (with minimums and daily limits).
  - **On-chain tasks** – Complete NFT or contract-based tasks for ALM (when enabled).
- **Lumina ID** and **airdrop** eligibility are designed to align with verified, active players when those programs are enabled. Staying active and following rules positions players for future rewards.

## Why AfroLumens, Why Now?

### 1. Telegram as the Platform

- **No app store friction** – Play inside Telegram; one tap from a link or bot.
- **Built-in audience** – Telegram’s growth and Mini App ecosystem make it easier to reach global and especially emerging-market users.
- **Identity and reach** – Optional Telegram username and referral links simplify invites and trust among friends.

### 2. Tap-to-Earn + Multiple Earning Paths

- **Low barrier** – Tapping is simple; anyone can start earning in seconds.
- **Depth** – Daily rewards, puzzles, mini-games, quizzes, tasks, and events keep engagement high without relying only on tapping.
- **Retention** – Energy, refills, weekly events, and leagues give reasons to return daily and weekly.

### 3. Community and Competition

- **Teams and leagues** turn individual play into group goals and rivalries.
- **Championships and stakes** (ALM in competitions) add real consequences and excitement.
- **Leaderboards and tiers** create visible progression and status.

### 4. Clear Path from In-App to On-Chain

- **ALM** is the in-app unit of value; **staking** and **marketplace** (ALM ↔ TON) link it to real value and ownership.
- **Wallet connection** and **Lumina ID** prepare players for airdrops and ecosystem benefits, making AfroLumens a bridge between casual play and crypto-aware users.

### 5. Sustainable Engagement, Not Just Clicks

- **Energy** and **refill limits** balance “tap all day” with “come back tomorrow.”
- **Staking** rewards medium- and long-term holders.
- **Tasks and events** reward attention and participation, not only raw taps.

Together, this makes AfroLumens timely as a Telegram-native, multi-activity, community-driven game with a path to real value and future airdrops.

## How Players Benefit

### 1. Earn From Day One

- **Tap** to earn ALM immediately; no purchase required.
- **Daily login**, **Decode**, **Matrix**, **Spinza**, **Quiz**, and **mini-games** add more ways to earn without paying.
- **Tasks** reward time (follow, watch, join) with ALM and LP/TP.

### 2. Grow Balance Through Strategy

- **Upgrade** multi-tap, energy, and mine to earn more per tap and per hour.
- **Stake** ALM to get principal + bonus at the end of the lock period.
- **Return regularly** to collect idle mine earnings (up to the idle cap) and use daily refills.

### 3. Compete and Climb

- **Level up** (Baobab → Mansa) for higher referral bonuses and status.
- **Join a team** to earn TP and take part in team challenges.
- **Join a league** (via your team) to earn LP, climb tiers (Bronze → Legend), and qualify for the **Championship**.
- **Create or join competitions** (team vs team, league vs league) to stake and win prize pools.

### 4. Invite and Earn More

- **Referral link** from the Friends tab; when friends join and accept terms, both sides get ALM.
- **Higher level** = bigger referral bonus.
- Referrals also count for **LP** in leagues, so they help you and your league.

### 5. Use ALM in the Ecosystem

- **Marketplace** – Sell ALM for TON or buy ALM with TON (with wallet connected).
- **Transfers** – Send ALM to friends or other players (within limits).
- **Donation** – Support charity with ALM.
- **Future** – Lumina ID and airdrops for eligible, active players when those programs are live.

### 6. One Place for Fun, Progress, and Community

- **Single app** – Game, Mine, Collection, Friends, Earn, Airdrop/Marketplace in one flow.
- **Weekly events** and **championships** give recurring goals.
- **Teams and leagues** add social accountability and shared goals.
